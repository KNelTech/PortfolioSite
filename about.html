About Me

                      
                      

                          Hey there, I'm Kodi Nelson—a tech-savvy repair specialist, developer, and all-around problem-solver. I thrive on tackling technical challenges head-on, whether I'm digging into some code, fixing hardware, or finding innovative solutions to keep things running smoothly.
                      

                      

                          My experience spans across development, troubleshooting, and cybersecurity, and I'm passionate about building efficient, straightforward solutions. I love challenges and am constantly looking for opportunities to learn new things, pushing myself to grow and adapt. I find great satisfaction in diving into complex problems and working through them step by step until I find the right answer. Whether it's learning a new programming language, mastering a new tool, or finding a better way to do something, I enjoy the constant evolution that comes with being in the tech field.
                      

                      

                          I'm also a big believer in the value of digital sovereignty and finding creative, secure ways to protect the future of communication and tech.
                      

                      

                          When I'm not in front of a screen, you might find me tinkering with a new gadget, diving into some security research, or hanging out with my girlfriend, Diana. I like to keep things real, solve tough problems, and share what I learn along the way.
                      

                      

                          I also love spending time outdoors, especially climbing mountains—I’ve climbed two in Colorado: Grays and Torreys. I practice martial arts as well, including MMA and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, which keep me focused and disciplined. These experiences help me come back to my work with a fresh perspective and new ideas.
